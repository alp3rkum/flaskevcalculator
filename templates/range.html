As EV (electric vehicle)'s are increasingly becoming a bigger part of transportation, there are numerous calculators related to various aspects of EV's. Among those aspects, their range remains the biggest concern. In a respect to this, Flask EV Range Calculator is designed to be the most consumer friendly calculator related to EV's.

Coded in HTML, CSS, Javascript and Python.

Feel free to toy around :)
